### List of components

- nike cap from a box
- orbit gum bottle
- a small cork piece for the panel angle
- upper part of a bottle
- 7x photoresistors
- 1x stepper motor of 5V
- 1x solar panel of 6V
- 12x father-father wires
- 20x mother-father wires
- 7x 10k Ohm resistors
- 4x 1.5V battery
- ULN2003APG driver
- Arduino Nano
